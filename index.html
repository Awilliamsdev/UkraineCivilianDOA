About

UkraineCivilianDAO is a DAO based system, decentralised community that will organise, fund community, supply purchases, deliveries, and financial, evacuation assistance. All evacuation is non aggressive and not carried out if it involves engaging in conflict. Conflict avoidance is the main priority of UkraineCivilianDAO.
